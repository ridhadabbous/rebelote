Rebelo ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you register as a driver, courier, or partner on our platform.

Information We Collect

When you register, we collect: your full name, email address, phone number, vehicle type, preferred working region or store location, and any additional information you provide during registration. We also collect the location data you provide by selecting a point on the map.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: process your registration, communicate with you about your account, match you with delivery or ride requests, improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
